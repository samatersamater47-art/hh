About PRIME MARKET

Electronics store ku yaala Hargeysa, Somaliland - Since 2015

Our Story / Taariikhdeena

PRIME MARKET waxa la aasaasay sanadkii 2015 magaalada Hargeysa, Somaliland. Waxa bilownay dukaan yar oo iibinaya qalabka elektarooniga ah, balse hadda waxa noqonay mid ka mid ah tafaariiqleyaasha ugu waaweyn ee qalabka casriga ah ee Somaliland.

Waxa ujeedadayadu tahay in aan Somaliland u keenano tiknoolojiyadii ugu dambeysay iyadoo la raacayo qiimo jaban oo macaamiishu awoodi karo. Waxa aaminsanahay in qof walba uu mudan yahay in uu helo qalab casri ah oo wax ku barasho, ganacsi iyo horumarkeedaba.

Founded in 2015, PRIME MARKET started as a small electronics shop in Hargeisa. Today, we are one of the leading electronics retailers in Somaliland, bringing you the latest technology at affordable prices.
